About Me

Hello and welcome to my personal website! My name is William, I'm 23 years old, and I am happy to have you here! I was born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia. I like dogs more than cats, basketball is my favorite sport, and my favorite food is pizza. In my free time, I like to play videogames, watch anime, and hang out with my friends. I’m also thinking about learning to play an instrument, the ukulele to be exact!

I have recently graduated from Georgia Southern University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I started my college career as a Mechanical Engineering major but found an interest in programming after working with a language called MATLAB. After my first year of school, I decided to switch my major to Computer Science and began my journey as a software developer. I learned the basics using Java and grew a passion for programming shortly after. I really enjoy the process of completing a programming project! Although it can be frustrating at times when my code doesn’t quite work, nothing beats the feeling of resolving my errors and seeing the finished product!

What Programming Means to Me

When I think about programming, I like to compare it to making a jigsaw puzzle. Most of the time, you start with a big idea that’s too much to handle all at once. So instead, we break the idea into smaller (puzzle) pieces. As we complete more and more pieces of the puzzle, we eventually end up with a finished product. At that point, I can’t help but think about how far we’ve come in the process and I grow an appreciation for the entire experience!
